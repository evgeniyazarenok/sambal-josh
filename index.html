2. Popularity of Indonesian/Balinese Spices

Hot sauce growth is driven by consumer interest in ethnic global flavors. Indonesian sambal is perfectly positioned within the "Asian/global" trend, benefiting from curiosity about authentic premium products.

Concrete signals of demand:

  • Indo Food Store: US-based leader with 1,000+ items.
  • Weee!: Major Asian e-commerce platform with dedicated Indonesian Sambal assortment.
  • Bali Marle: An artisan brand demonstrating that sambal can win awards and command premium pricing in the West.

3. Amazon & Online Competition

Amazon remains the dominant channel for specialty spices. While mainstream brands (Tabasco, Cholula) dominate volume, the "Indonesian Sambal" niche is growing but remains under-represented.

  • "Indonesian Sambal": Numbers in the dozens of SKUs.
  • "Balinese Sambal": Extremely rare, accounting for less than 1% of the category.

This represents a massive "Blue Ocean" opportunity for Sambal Josh to own the Balinese heat segment.
